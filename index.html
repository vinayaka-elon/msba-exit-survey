How well did we prepare you?

You're about to graduate from the MSBA program. Before you head off, I want to know how it actually went for you. What worked, what didn't, where you feel ready, and where you don't.

Your honest feedback genuinely shapes the program. Every year we revise the curriculum based on what graduating students tell us in this survey. The bootcamp format, the elective lineup, the way we teach a bunch of these classes, all of it has changed because of student responses. So if something didn't work, please say so. If something really did, that helps too.

It takes about seven minutes. Thanks for taking the time.
